OUR Business Concept

Broom of Africa (Pty) Ltd is a cleaning and green environment company which aims at ensuring a clean and safe ambiance at all areas; from residential to commercial spaces. This includes transformation of unused spaces into green and clean environments by means of planting trees, bushes and decorative gardens and promotion of environmental health and sanitation. It also aims to be a retailer of sanitation products through specialised stores, and a retailer of household/ outdoor ornamental pieces.
