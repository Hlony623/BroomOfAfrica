Cleaning Services




Currently working a project of Cleaning Services of 12 Health Centres in Quthing, Qacha snek and Thaba Tseka district under Lesotho Millennium Development Agency.

AREAS TO BE CLEANED:

1. Waiting areas

2. Consulting rooms

3. Counselling rooms

4. Delivery rooms TB Shelter

5. Observation rooms

6. Pharmacy and Pharmacy store

7. Multipurpose hall

8. Waiting mothers

9. Ventilated Improve Pit latrines Toilets and Ablutions Passages





DAILY CLEANING ACTIVITIES:


1. Sweep and wet mop all floors

2. Dust tables, chairs and cabinets

3. Disinfect all regularly touched areas: door handles

4. Clean toilets

5. Change refuse bags





WEEKLY CLEANING ACTIVITIES:


1. Floor stripping

2. Applying polish

3. Clean windows

4. Clean toilets

5. Clean walls
